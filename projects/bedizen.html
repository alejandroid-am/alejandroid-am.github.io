BEDiZEN

This project emerged as part of my training as UI Designer at CCI Campus Alsace. It is a startup called BEDiZEN (an acronym for Beyond Digital Zenith) dedicated to providing innovative Augmented Reality solutions tailored for small and medium-sized e-commerce businesses, helping them enhance customer experience and engagement.

Role: UX/UI Designer, Branding
Tools: Figma, Illustrator, HTML/CSS
Year: 2024-Present

The Challenge

BEDiZEN was born with a clear mission: to make Web Augmented Reality (WebAR) accessible to small and medium e-commerce businesses, especially in fashion and beauty. The challenge was to design a bold, human, and technically forward identity that reflects a young startup ready to push digital boundaries — true to its name: Beyond Digital Zenith.

My Process

1 - Research & Discovery

The project began with an in-depth research phase: market analysis, visual trends, and technology benchmarks. I studied how existing WebXR and AR services communicated innovation, then defined BEDiZEN’s own positioning — modern, minimal, and focused on experience rather than just technology.

2 - Moodboard & Explorations

The identity work started with the logo, built from Code Next Extra Bold and customized for balance and recognizability. Its geometric structure conveys stability and movement, while the tagline “Beyond Digital Zenith” anchors the brand’s purpose. The color palette — anthracite gray (#1A1B1C), light gray (#F2F1F0), and accent red (#E13C30) — creates a sense of elegance and energy. The red dot on the “i” of BEDiZEN became a subtle signature element: a spark of creativity within the structure.
