Spin! App

Spin is a Top-Up and E-Gift mobile app that turns international recharges into a fun and rewarding experience. After each transaction, users spin a wheel of fortune to earn points, coins, vouchers, or bonus recharges making what used to be a simple utility feel like a small celebration.

Role: UI/UX Designer, Visual Identity Refresh
Tools: Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop
Year: 2025

The Challenge

The original Spin app was functional but visually outdated and inconsistent. Users completed their top-ups but with a fairly high learning curve and rarely engaged with the gamified elements. The challenge was to create a more enjoyable and emotionally engaging user flow and experience without complicating the flow.

My Process

1 — Understanding the Flow

I started by mapping the complete user flow from country selection to payment and post-transaction rewards. The process, originally fragmented, needed smoother transitions and clearer feedback moments. Using Yu-Kai Chou’s Octalysis Framework, I identified motivation opportunities in “Development & Accomplishment,” “Unpredictability,” and “Ownership.”

2 — Research & Moodboard

I explored color and motion trends from fintech and mobile gaming apps, looking for ways to blend trust with playfulness. Typography shifted to a more geometric sans-serif to enhance clarity.

3 — Redefining the Visual Identity

The logo was simplified for scalability and motion integration. The new “SPIN” wordmark maintains circular movement in its negative space — a visual nod to the app’s reward wheel. The icon system became lighter and more rounded, aligning with a positive and human tone.

4 — UI Redesign & Microinteractions

I kept many of the current conventions, mainly in the navigation bar and iconography, and also included its new identity, which will allow for faster acceptance among users.

5 — Gamification Improvements

In addition to the app's key gameplay element, the wheel of fortune, the best way I thought was appropriate to gamify the experience was through micro-interactions. Fluid animations and haptic feedback in some interactions are Spin's best allies.
