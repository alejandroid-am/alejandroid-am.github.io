WorkPics

A UI/UX concept for a mobile app designed to simplify photo and geolocation logging of progress for physical jobs like construction or cleaning.

Role: UI/UX Designer, Visual Identity
Tools: Figma, Illustrator, Paper & Pencil
Year: 2025 (Conceptual)

The Problem: Inefficient Field Documentation

Logging the progress of physical work (construction, cleaning, maintenance) often involves error-prone manual processes: taking photos, noting locations, sending reports... This consumes valuable field staff time and can lead to inconsistencies.

WorkPics originated from the need for an extremely simple tool: Open -> Shoot -> Done. The goal was to minimize friction and cognitive load for users who need to document their work quickly, ensuring that information (photo, time, location) is captured and synced automatically and reliably.

My Process: Radical Simplification

1 — Zoning and Minimal Flow

The process began on paper, defining the most direct user flow possible. The premise was: **fewer clicks, more automation**. Different layouts (Zoning) were explored to find the most intuitive arrangement for the primary action: taking the photo. Geolocation and synchronization were designed to happen in the background, requiring no user intervention.

2 — Prototyping in Figma

The zoning was translated into Figma, creating low-fidelity wireframes and then an interactive prototype. The design focused on a prominent camera button and a clean interface displaying only essential information. The flow was refined to ensure the minimum possible steps from opening the app to having the photo synced.

Try Figma Prototype

3 — Visual Identity and Logos

In parallel, a mini Brand Style Guide was developed, defining a clear, legible typeface (e.g., Helvetica or similar) and a functional color palette with good contrast for outdoor visibility. Two final logo proposals were explored, aiming to convey efficiency and connection (work + cloud/sync), along with a provisional logo/icon used during development for quick app representation.
