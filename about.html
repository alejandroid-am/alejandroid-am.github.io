Hello, again.

My journey has been a constant evolution: from crafting 3D virtual worlds to designing digital experiences that connect with people.

I aim to build fluid interfaces and cohesive visual identities, but above all, to create accessible and meaningful experiences. I’m passionate about exploring how technology, from XR to AI, can be used to innovate in an ethical, human-centered way.

A few fun facts

Cocktail Alchemist

I genuinely enjoy mixing cocktails. For me, it feels like brewing modern-day potions, where every ingredient is key to creating a small, magical experience.

Explorer on Wheels

Cyclotourism is my form of moving meditation. This year I completed the Vélodyssée, a 400km+ adventure that proved the best views are discovered at a slower pace.

Keeper of Memories

I collect coins from the places I visit; each one is a tangible memory. My favorite is a special edition from Hogwarts. I'm still waiting for my letter, but that coin reminds me that magic is in the details.

A Global Perspective

Living in Cuba, Chile, and France has gifted me a broader visual and reflective spectrum. This cultural interplay enriches my design approach, allowing me to create with greater empathy and a cosmopolitan outlook.
