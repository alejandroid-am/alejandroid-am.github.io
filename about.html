Hello, again.

Visual & UI Designer based in Strasbourg.

Am I the right person for your project?
Let’s find out.

I don't have a standard background, and honestly? That is my biggest asset.

My design process is shaped by a life of constant reinvention. I learned resilience in Cuba, business strategy in Chile—where I co-founded Somos Arte and built a community of 10k+ people from scratch—and finally, adaptability in France, where I moved for love and mastered a third language.

Why does this matter to you?
Because I don't look at a project and just see colors and typography. Thanks to my entrepreneurial roots, I see business goals, constraints, and user needs. I understand that a design isn't finished when it looks pretty in Figma; it's finished when it works in the browser and converts for your business.

I bridge the gap between creative vision and technical reality. Whether it's coding a prototype in Tailwind, modeling in Blender, or defining a brand identity, I bring a "founder's mindset" to every pixel.

I’m not just looking for a task to complete; I’m looking for the next problem to solve.

So, shall we work together?
