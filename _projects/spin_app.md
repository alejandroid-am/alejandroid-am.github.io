## The Business Problem
Spin App allows users to top up mobile credit, but it was failing to retain users. The retention rate was low, the branding felt "cheap," and the core gamification mechanic was actually discouraging users from spending money.

## The Mechanics: Fixing the Reward Loop
The original flow was broken: users spun the wheel *before* paying. If they got a "bad" result (like 0 rewards), they simply didn't complete the transaction.

**My Strategy:**
I inverted the flow. Now, the "Spin" is a reward *after* the payment is validated. 
* **Always-Win System:** We replaced the punishing "Skull" icon (which meant zero reward) with a point system. Even if you don't win the jackpot, you earn points or discounts.
* **Psychological Impact:** The user leaves the app on a high note, feeling rewarded, which drastically increases the likelihood of returning.

I redesigned the mascot to be more expressive and versatile for social media, along with a completely new 6-color palette that feels trustworthy yet playful.
